About us



Who We Are

Travame, pronounced as "Travel-me", stands for my travels and me. We are 3 enthusiastic students from National University of Singapore (NUS) who wish to automate trip itineraries planning through our app GoTnT (Go Trip and Travel) via Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning and Data Mining using Microsoft Azure.



What We Do

We believe with the power and knowledge from School of Design and Environment (SDE) and School of Computing (SoC), we would make the trip planning as fun as travelling and we will take over the travel world ONE DAY! :)

Our Services & Technology

Automated Travel Itinerary Planner

Generates and customizes your personalized trip itineraries automatically to save your precious time from planning a trip manually.

Special Needs Itinerary planning

Specially cater to plan itinerary for people with special needs.

Personal Travel Assistant

Provides all-in-one travel services with interactive chat bot that solves your enquiries and lighten up your day.

Virtual Tour

Go on a virtual tour of the planned itinerary!

Accessibility Objects Recognition

Our state-of-art techonology will keep you informed of the latest accessibility status to all attractions

Data Mining

Get the latest up-to-date places of interest with our technology!

Group Travel Collaborator

Organizes the trip for you and your companions automatically by matching the needs of all participants to enhance your travel experience.

Attractions Advisor

Recommends must-go attractions and places of interests to enlighten your travel moment.

World Explorer - Virtual Passport for Travel Enthusiast

Collects unique stamps from well-known attractions all over the world in your personal virtual passport.

Trip Budget Tracker

Reviews and monitors your travel budget to optimize your expenses easily.

Travel Gamer - In travel gamification Fun & Play

Enjoys the travel while collecting experience points, unlocking badges and discovering new insights.

Trip Diary

Records and converts your travel memories into interesting diary entries automatically.
